Our Story

Family Recipe,
Honest Food

Warung Kopi Setia started in 2008 as a small stall by Mak Cik Rosnah, serving her late mother's recipes to the neighbourhood. Today, we're a full restaurant but the heart remains the same — real Malaysian food made with love.

Every dish uses fresh market ingredients sourced daily. No shortcuts, no preservatives — just the taste you grew up with.
