About Us

Who We Are

LHNS Engineering Trading is a trusted technical services company based in Melaka and Nilai, Malaysia. We specialize in solar panel installation, electrical wiring, air conditioning systems, and CCTV security solutions.

With branches serving Melaka, Nilai, and surrounding areas in Negeri Sembilan, we provide end-to-end solutions — from consultation and supply to installation, troubleshooting, and ongoing maintenance.

We serve homeowners, offices, shops, and small businesses, delivering fast, reliable, and professional service at every step.
